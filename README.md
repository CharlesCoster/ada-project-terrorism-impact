# Research questions
With importance of terrorism nowadays, many questions arise on different aspect of the citizen life. 
- **Political context** : the political context in a country reflect in part the attitude of citizen towards foreigners. Do we assist to a rise of extremist political parties ? We will interest in the evolution of governements in european countries and in the political belief of citizen.
- **Economic context** : the economic growth is linked to leisures, in particular according to art, textile and touristic industries. Do we observe a significant economic recession in these sectors? We will interest on different economic indicator related to leisure of citizen.
- **Religious context** : in difficult times, people are prone to turn to religion because they need something to believe in. Moreover, many terrorism attack are religious. How does the religious affiliation evolve in terrorism period? We will interest on sentiment of religious belief and religious habits.

- [Comparative Political Data Set](http://www.cpds-data.org/index.php/data) contains annual political and institutional data for 36 european countries (1960-2017). - [Europe Political Affiliation](https://europeelects.eu/data/) provides information on the european political affiliation from  to per country according to different survey. Data will be collected using web scrapping.
